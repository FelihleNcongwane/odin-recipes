Peach Avocado Salsa

Do You Know Salsa

This is usually a spicy sauce of chopped tomatoes, onions and peppers that is commonly served with Mexican food. Also, it can be a similar sauce made with a main ingredient other than tomatoes.

Here we are talking about avocado salsa with fresh peaches, bell pepper, red onion, and spicy jalapeno making a delicious summertime dip. Also great as a topper for grilled chicken or fish. The longer it can marinate before you add the avocado, the better it is.

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh peaches - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • ½ red onion, minced
  • ½ red bell pepper, minced
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced

Directions

  1. Gently mix peaches, jalapeño, onion, bell pepper, cilantro, garlic, lime juice, and lemon juice in a bowl; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Fold avocado into salsa to serve.
